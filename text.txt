Defines the loop mode for all animations.
[p] [/p][p] [/p]
[b]Loop None[/b]: The animation will stop on the last frame.
[p] [/p][p] [/p]
[b]Loop Linear[/b]: The animation will repeat once it reaches the last frame.
[p] [/p][p] [/p]
[b]Loop PingPong[/b]: The animation will play in reverse when it reaches the last frame, and  vice versa.
